Visitors and workers were cleared out of Manhattan’s Trump Tower Tuesday afternoon shortly before 5 p. m. after a suspicious package was reported to the New York City Police Department, according to multiple media reports.[  Donald Trump and his family are staying at their    estate and compound in Palm Beach, Florida for a Christmas break.
