In the “Saturday Night Live” season 42 finale, Alec Baldwin playing the role of President Donald Trump sang Leonard Cohen’s “Hallelujah,” the same song SNL had Kate McKinnon cover in her Hillary Clinton role in the show after she lost the election to Trump.
