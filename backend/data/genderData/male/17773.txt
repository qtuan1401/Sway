SARASOTA, Florida  —   Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump will spend the last day before Election Day campaigning in five key battleground states, using every moment and every last bit of strength remaining in an effort to win the 2016 election. NORTH CAROLINA  —   Trump will hold a rally in Raleigh at the J. S. Norton arena at 3:00 p. m. EST. The event marks the second in North Carolina in the past five days.
