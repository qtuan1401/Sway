Jason Johnson, political editor for The Root, addressed the polls showing that Donald Trump is only getting two percent of   voters, and reasoned the numbers are so low for Trump because the GOP presidential nominee has associated himself with the Ku Klux Klan and alienated  .  “The problem is Donald Trump has associated himself with the Ku Klux Klan.
