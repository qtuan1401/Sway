Friday before the inauguration, ESPN aired a piece promoting now President Donald Trump as the new “First Fan in Chief” who “could have more connections to sports than any other. ”  ESPN noted how Trump in 2004 told former “SportsCenter” host Dan Patrick he would run for president before being a principal owner of a professional sports team and how Trump combined his love of golf and real estate background to build golf courses.
