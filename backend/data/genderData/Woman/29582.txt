GOP frontrunner Donald Trump agreed to sit down for an interview with Fox News host Megyn Kelly on May 17th, according to Page Six. [The agreement comes after Kelly recently met Trump at Trump Tower in New York earlier this month.  Trump has sparred with Kelly after saying she treated him unfairly during the first GOP primary debate last August when Kelly asked, “You’ve called women you don’t like ‘fat pigs,’ ‘dogs,’ ‘slobs’ and ‘disgusting animals,’” adding, “Does that sound to you like the temperament of a man we should elect as president?”
