Reuters covers a Donald Trump Rally in South Carolina:  Republican presidential hopeful Donald Trump filled a hotel ballroom with over 2, 000 people for a rally on Wednesday, attracting   supporters as he has in other cities, but was dogged by a rival who is also very popular in South Carolina: Ted Cruz. A dozen people interviewed by Reuters said the Texas senator offered a strong alternative to the   New York businessman, though more than half still planned to vote for Trump. “If he doesn’t drop out of the race, I’m definitely going to vote for him,” Daniel Barrett, 48, said of Trump. When asked why he added the caveat about Trump staying in the race, Barrett said: “He might be rallying the troops for someone else, for Cruz. ” Read the whole thing.
