Breitbart News ranked #1 in the world for most shared Facebook content in the 24 hours surrounding the third presidential debate, besting number two by over 150, 000 Facebook shares. Number two on the list was the Donald Trump for President Facebook page, which achieved 362, 268 shares during the same time period.  The next closest media publishers to Breitbart News were Fox News at #8 (185, 119 shares) and Good Morning America at #10 (144, 663 shares). Huffington Post came in #14 with 114, 289 shares CNN ranked #28 with just 78, 539 shares.
